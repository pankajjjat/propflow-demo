🔒Ecosystem Lock-In — Switching Costs That Compound

5 switching costs that make leaving unthinkable as usage deepens.

  1. Historical Deal Data
    6+ months of deal history, client documents, AI analysis, WhatsApp conversations. A broker cannot just "switch" and lose this. Every month of usage increases switching cost exponentially.
  2. Client-Side Accounts
    Clients create accounts to track their documents. They expect their broker to be on PropFlow. If a broker leaves, they can't take the client-side experience with them.
  3. Registration Templates
    Custom templates for each state's sub-registrar (different document requirements per state). Broker's entire registration process is configured — starting over means contacting every client manually.
  4. WhatsApp Template Approvals
    Meta-approved HSM message templates take 2-4 weeks to approve. A competitor would need to re-submit and wait. Meanwhile, PropFlow's templates keep working.
  5. Custom Checklist Templates
    Each broker builds their own checklist library for different deal types (apartment, villa, commercial, plot, rental) with their specific document requirements. This library is personal and extensive.
